Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a fun, interactive, and informative website about Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra.

Audience

The target audience are Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra fans that are between the ages of 10 and 60 years of age.

Dynamic elements

I will include various different Javascript elements. I will make a Avatar quote generator that randomly choses from an array of Avatar Quotes. There will be key value pairs linked with every character. There will be a lot of pictures on the "Character Deep Dive" page. So I will make the pictures become big screen pictures that you can view, if you click on them. I also want to inlcude a registration page for people to register as a bender member. I will also add a character information page with a working search bar for people to look up information about Avatar characters. Another page I plan to include, is a Avatar Trivia quiz. I plan to just code the frontend code, which means the answers will be available in the source code. If the quiz is a little too hard to make, than I won't include that part.

Character Deep Dives

Aang, the central character of Avatar: The Last Airbender, is a complex and beloved figure. He is the Avatar, a reincarnation cycle destined to maintain balance in the world. As the Avatar, Aang possesses the ability to bend all four elements: air, water, earth, and fire.

Aang's True Potential: Some fans speculate that Aang never fully realized his potential as the Avatar. While he mastered the four elements, there's a belief that he could have achieved even greater heights, perhaps even bending energy itself. The Air Nomad Legacy: Aang's connection to the Air Nomads and their culture is a significant aspect of his character. Some theories suggest that he may have had a deeper understanding of Air Nomad history and philosophy than initially portrayed. The Avatar State: Aang's Avatar State is a powerful transformation that grants him immense strength and skill. Some fans theorize that he could have learned to control this state more precisely, allowing him to utilize its power without losing consciousness. Some fans speculate that Aang may have had a unique connection to the Moon Spirit, influencing his waterbending abilities.
